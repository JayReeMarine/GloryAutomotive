About Our Workshop

Welcome to Glory Automotive — your trusted partner for quality vehicle repairs and servicing in Oakleigh and Melbourne’s eastern suburbs. Established in 2015, our workshop is led by Jay and his team of skilled mechanics who treat every vehicle like their own. We pride ourselves on delivering professional, hassle-free service to every customer.

Our services include everything from logbook servicing and roadworthy inspections to complex mechanical repairs. We also offer convenient options like loan cars during extended repairs, and our workshop is located within walking distance of public transport and Oakleigh Central shopping precinct.

Whatever your automotive needs, we're here to help. Contact us today to find out how we can keep your vehicle running at its best.

About Our Team

Glory Automotive was founded by Jay Moon, a passionate mechanic who moved to Melbourne from South Korea in 2007. Jay’s love for cars began in his childhood, and he honed his expertise by studying automotive mechanics in both Korea and Australia. His hands-on experience includes working with top dealerships such as Chrysler Jeep and BMW Brighton, beginning in 2008.

With over a decade of experience, Jay established Glory Automotive in 2015, assembling a strong and reliable team. Today, the Glory Automotive family includes:

  • Noah – With 3 years of experience, Noah has been with Glory since its inception and continues to provide expert mechanical care.
  • Josh – A trusted member of the team since 2016, Josh brings 2 years of hands-on experience and customer-first dedication.
  • Take and Luke – The newest additions to the team, both joined in 2017 and have become integral to daily operations.

Together, our team is committed to delivering exceptional service and building long-term relationships with every customer.
